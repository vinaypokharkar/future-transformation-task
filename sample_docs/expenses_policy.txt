Acme Corporation — Employee Expenses Policy

Purpose

This policy explains how staff should handle money spent on behalf of the
company, and how that money is returned to them.

Reimbursement Window

Employees may request reimbursement within 30 days of purchase. Requests
submitted after this window require written approval from a department head,
and may be declined. Receipts must be attached to every request; a bank
statement line is not sufficient evidence on its own.

Travel

Standard-class rail and economy-class air fares are covered in full. Upgrades
are the personal responsibility of the traveller unless the flight exceeds
eight hours, in which case premium economy is permitted. Taxi fares are
covered where public transport is unavailable or unsafe.

Accommodation

Hotel stays are capped at 180 GBP per night in London and 120 GBP per night
elsewhere in the United Kingdom. Where a conference offers a discounted room
rate, staff are expected to use it.

Meals

A daily allowance of 40 GBP applies while travelling on company business.
Alcohol is not covered. Client entertainment is handled separately and must be
approved in advance by a director.

Equipment

Purchases of office equipment above 250 GBP require prior approval. Software
subscriptions must be procured through the IT department rather than expensed
individually, so that licences remain centrally managed.

Disputes

If a request is declined, staff may escalate to the finance team within 14
days. The finance team aims to respond to escalations within five working days.
